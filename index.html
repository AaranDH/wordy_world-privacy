🌍 Wordy World

Privacy Policy

👋 Introduction

Welcome to Wordy World, a word search game developed by Aaran Hodder, based in the United Kingdom. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

By using Wordy World, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

👶 Children's Privacy COPPA

Wordy World is designed for children aged 5 and above. We take children's privacy very seriously and comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the UK GDPR.

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • We do not require account registration or sign-in
  • We do not allow children to share personal information within the app
  • All advertising is configured with child-directed treatment enabled
  • Ad content is restricted to family-friendly ratings only (G-rated)

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

📱 Information We Collect

Wordy World is designed to minimise data collection. Here is what we collect and why:

  • Game progress data — Your scores, stars, completed levels, achievements and settings are stored locally on your device only using shared preferences storage. This data never leaves your device.
  • Advertising data — We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve ads. This is subject to Google's privacy policy.

We do not collect your name, email address, location, or any other personally identifiable information.

📢 Advertising

Wordy World contains advertisements provided by Google AdMob. We have configured AdMob with the following child safety settings:

  • Child-directed treatment is enabled
  • Maximum ad content rating is set to G (General audiences)
  • Ads are family-friendly and appropriate for all ages

Advertising helps us keep Wordy World free. Players can remove all ads via a one-time in-app purchase. For more information about how Google uses data from apps that use AdMob, please visit Google's Privacy Policy.

💾 Data Storage

All game data including your progress, stars, achievements, themes and settings is stored locally on your device. This data is:

  • Never transmitted to our servers
  • Never shared with third parties (except as described in the Advertising section)
  • Deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Resettable at any time via Settings → Reset All Progress

🔒 In-App Purchases

Wordy World offers optional in-app purchases processed by Google Play. We do not handle or store any payment information. All transactions are managed securely by Google Play's billing system, subject to Google's terms and privacy policy.

🇬🇧 UK GDPR & Your Rights

If you are based in the United Kingdom or European Economic Area, you have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to access — You can view all your game data within the app
  • Right to deletion — You can delete all data via Settings → Reset All Progress, or by uninstalling the app
  • Right to portability — Your data is stored locally on your device and is under your control

As we do not collect personal data on our servers, most GDPR rights are exercised directly on your device.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
